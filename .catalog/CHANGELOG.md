## 2.12

- Added "Created by Coolshrimp" to the About screen.
- Renamed the Settings output option from `MAX98357A` to `External` for clarity.
- Documented that any MP3 bitrate (8-320 kbps, CBR and VBR) plays.
- Documented Now Playing controls: Up/Down volume, Left/Right track change, hold Left/Right to seek, OK play/pause, Back to return.
- Clarified that internal playback is quiet and low fidelity by hardware limitation, and that the external MAX98357A module is strongly recommended and keeps full volume control.
- Added screenshots and an MIT license.
